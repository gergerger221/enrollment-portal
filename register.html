Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information including name, email, phone number, date of birth, and address for enrollment purposes. We also collect academic records and documents submitted during the application process.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process applications, communicate about enrollment status, provide academic services, and improve our educational programs. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All data is encrypted and stored securely.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

5. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact privacy@academyofbiringan.edu

Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using our enrollment system, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use our services.

2. Enrollment Requirements

Students must meet all admission requirements and provide accurate information. False information may result in rejection of application or dismissal from the Academy of Biringan.

3. Academic Integrity

All students must maintain academic honesty. Cheating, plagiarism, or any form of academic dishonesty will result in disciplinary action.

4. Payment Obligations

Students are responsible for timely payment of tuition and fees. Late payments may incur penalties and affect enrollment status.

5. Code of Conduct

All students must adhere to the Academy of Biringan's code of conduct both on and off academy. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide better user experience, analyze site usage, and assist in our marketing efforts.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use essential cookies for site functionality, analytical cookies to understand user behavior, and marketing cookies to personalize your experience.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your behalf. These include analytics tools and social media integrations.

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

2. Accessibility Features

Our site includes features such as keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility, color contrast compliance, and resizable text to accommodate users with different needs.
